Strawberry Pie

Description

A cloud of sweet summer bliss.

A buttery, flaky crust cradles a jewel-toned cascade of ripe, juicy strawberries, bathed in a sun-kissed glaze.

Each tender bite is a burst of fruity perfection, leaving behind a whisper of vanilla and a lingering smile.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Cold & normal butter
  • Ice water
  • Fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • White sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt.
  2. Using a pastry blender or two knives, cut the cold butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough just comes together. Be careful not to over-mix.
  4. Divide the dough in half, shape into disks, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, flour, cinnamon, cornstarch and salt. Gently stir in the sliced strawberries and lemon juice
  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one of the chilled dough disks to a 12-inch diameter. Fit it into a 9-inch pie dish, trimming the edges as needed.
  7. Pour the strawberry filling into the prepared pie crust. Dot with butter pieces.
  8. Roll out the second dough disk and place it over the filling. Trim the edges, crimp, and cut slits in the top crust for venting.

  10. Let the pie cool completely on a wire rack before serving.
